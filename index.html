Case Studies

Case Study 1: Commercial Termite Infestation in Etobicoke Trucking Yard

A corporate property near The Queensway and Islington Avenue faced unexpected termite damage. The owners contacted our expert exterminators after noticing sagging wood and pellet-like droppings.

Our team performed a complete inspection, identified subterranean termites, and installed baiting systems throughout the structure. After three months of monitoring, termite activity ceased entirely. Integrated treatment and ongoing care preserved the building without harmful disruption to operations.

Case Study 2: Bedbug Outbreak in a Scarborough Apartment Tower

At a high-rise near Kennedy Station, a resident reported persistent bedbug bites despite prior treatments. Our experienced technicians conducted a comprehensive inspection across six units and identified a widespread infestation.

Using heat treatments, safe sprays, and tenant cooperation, our extermination team successfully eliminated the infestation. Post-treatment follow-up programs and tenant education ensured prevention of future outbreaks.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much should I expect to pay for removal services?

Pest control costs vary depending on the type of pest and severity of infestation. Our pricing ranges from $150-$300 for ants, $200-$500 for rodents, $500-$1,500 for bed bugs, and $1,000-$2,500 for termites. We provide free quotes for accurate pricing specific to your situation.

What are the different types of services available?

We offer comprehensive pest control services including chemical control for quick elimination, biological control using natural predators, mechanical control with traps and barriers, and Integrated Pest Management (IPM) for long-term sustainable solutions.

What is the difference between pest control and exterminator?

Pest control refers to the broader management and prevention of pests through various methods, while an exterminator specifically focuses on eliminating existing pest infestations. Our services encompass both approaches for comprehensive pest management.

What is the best time of year to get rid of rats?

Fall and early winter are typically the best times for rodent control, as rats seek shelter indoors during colder months. However, we provide year-round services and 24/7 emergency response for immediate infestations regardless of season.

What are some tips for preventing mice in my home?

Key prevention tips include: storing food in airtight containers, maintaining cleanliness especially in kitchens, sealing entry points, removing clutter, trimming vegetation away from your home, and conducting regular property inspections for signs of activity.
